Shaping Infrastructure Since 2017

CHAVAN PATIL & ASSOCIATE Civil Engineering was founded with a simple mission: to build infrastructure that stands the test of time. Over nearly three decades, we have grown from a small local firm to one of the region's most respected construction companies.

Our team of licensed civil engineers, project managers, and skilled craftsmen work together to deliver roads, bridges, commercial structures, and residential projects with unmatched quality.
